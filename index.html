Last Updated: April 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you browse websites. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

EduTech Platform uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our platform, such as logging into your student or admin account and securely processing checkout items in your cart.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our courses and resources so we can improve the user experience.

3. Managing Your Preferences

You can set your browser not to accept cookies. However, in a few cases, some of our platform features (like saving items to your shopping cart or keeping you logged in) may not function as a result.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an EduTech student account, we collect personal information such as your Full Name and a valid @gmail.com email address. When you purchase books from our bookstore, we collect shipping details including your address and preferred shipping method.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Create and manage your academic account.
  • Process your bookstore orders and deliver your items.
  • Notify you about relevant academic calendar events, course updates, and news.

3. Data Security

EduTech uses industry-standard encryption (such as bcrypt for password hashing) to protect your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it. For any data-related requests, please contact the EduTech Administration team.
